About us

Froggers Esports

Our team, Vortex Gaming, has set several goals that we aim to achieve in the competitive gaming industry. First and foremost, our primary goal is to win tournaments and competitions in popular multiplayer games such as Valorant and Apex Legends.

In addition to winning, we want to build a strong fan base and establish a recognizable brand by engaging with our fans on social media, streaming platforms, and other channels. We believe that building a strong fan base will not only help us grow our brand, but also motivate us to perform at our best during competitions.

We also aim to support the growth of the esports industry by participating in community events, attending industry conferences, and partnering with other organizations. By doing so, we can help promote the growth and development of esports.

Finally, we want to provide opportunities for aspiring esports players to join our team and improve their skills. We believe that by providing a platform for talented gamers to showcase their abilities, we can contribute to the growth of the industry and provide more opportunities for players to succeed.
